Best Skill

The best skill of all of these is Eminem. Eminem is often praised as one of the most skilled rappers ever because of his crazy rhyme schemes, rapid flows, and ability to bend words so they still rhyme perfectly. He packs multiple internal rhymes into almost every bar, switches cadences easily, and uses clever wordplay and metaphors that you catch only after listening a few times. On top of the technical side, he’s known for powerful storytelling and emotional songs that make his verses hit even harder, which is why many fans and critics rank him among the greatest in hip-hop history.
