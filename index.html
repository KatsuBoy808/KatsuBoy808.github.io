The Top 5 Wealthiest Rappers

The rap game has a lot of people in it, but who are the wealthiest?

5.

Eminem - With successful albums and tours, alongside business endeavors, Eminem's net worth is estimated to be around $250 million. With work going all the way back to his "Slim Shady" days he is one of the most respected and wealthiest rappers out there.

4.

Kanye West - With ventures in music, fashion, and various business enterprises, Kanye's net worth has fluctuated, but estimates put it around $400 million. Even though he put himself through lots of controversy he still remains one of the most successful and wealthiest rappers.

3.

Dr. Dre - Known for his contributions to hip-hop and the Beats by Dre brand, Dr. Dre's net worth is estimated to be around $800 million.
